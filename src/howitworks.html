How does Project:Gaia work?

Look for the item needed and Reserve.


Start by browsing our website to find the item you're interested in renting. Take your time to compare options and select the perfect item that meets your needs.

Go to the shop to Pick up Reserved item


Once reservation is done online, head to the designated shop location for pickup. make sure bring any necessary identification or confirmation details to ensure a smooth collection process.

Wear and Enjoy!


After picking up the reserved item, It's time to enjoy! Use it as planned, whether it's for a special event, a casual outing, or any other occasion. Make the most of your rental and have fun!

Return on Time


When you're finished, Please return the item to the shop by the agreed-upon date and time. Timely returns help the shops maintain inventory and provide great service to all clients. If you need an extension, reach out to the shop you reserved from in advance to see if it is possible.
